Public Seminars

We are not just your average online coding course.

When you participate in one of our public seminars, you get:

Small class sizes

You'll be in a class with no more than 12 participants. That means lots of opportunity to speak directly with the instructor.

Engaging and experienced instructors

All our instructors are experts in their field. But more importantly, they have extensive teaching experience to individuals with varying levels of technical expertise.

Access to resources

When you register for a seminar, your learning does not begin and end in class. You'll also be provided with software installation guides, relevant video tutorials, and have access to a post-seminar office hour sessions with the instructor.

Certificate of Completion

All participants will receive a snazzy certificate at the end of the seminar.

Stay in the loop!

Get notified when we have upcoming public online and in-person seminars.
